When does the Catonsville edition mail, and how many homes? +

Each edition is printed 9×12 and mailed to 2,500 Catonsville homes on a roughly six-week cycle — about eight mailings a year. You can reserve a single mailing or commit across the year.

What's included with a placement? +

Every spot pairs a printed placement on the card with a focused digital landing page, one local offer, and a QR path from print to your page. Higher tiers add priority listing, more space, and social features.

Do I have to provide my own photography? +

Helpful, but not required. Send what you have during intake. Where a photo isn't ready, we set your business name in the guide's editorial style until artwork is in hand — your listing never looks unfinished.

How does pricing and payment work? +

Spots start at $295 per mailing. After we confirm fit and category availability, we send a proof and a payment link — there's no charge before you've seen and approved your placement.

Can I reserve my category so a competitor can't appear? +

Quad Sponsors can hold category exclusivity for an edition. Other tiers are first-come within each category, so the earlier you reserve, the better your position.
