# Business Plan

## Concept

Maryland Local Picks combines a curated local print card with a digital city
guide and dedicated advertiser landing pages. Catonsville is the first market.

Residents receive a concise set of nearby businesses, useful offers, a Pick of
the Week, and an optional local newsletter. Advertisers receive print
placement, a conversion-focused digital page, city directory placement, a QR
destination, and a clear local offer.

## Initial Product

The launch message should stay simple:

**Get featured locally in print and online.**

The first card is expected to support half, single, double, and quad
placements. Print placements are paid upfront and include the digital page.
Final pricing, route counts, quantity, and dates remain campaign decisions.

## Revenue Path

1. One-time print placements
2. Recurring placements on an approximately six-week cadence
3. Prepaid multi-mailing packages
4. Digital-only listing downsells
5. Paid Pick of the Week and social promotion
6. SignalWire call tracking
7. Later website, reputation, advertising, and AI answering services

Bonuses such as weekly features, offer updates, placement priority, and
category protection should be favored over deep discounts.

## Launch Test

The first edition should prove that businesses will pay, the offer is easy to
understand, the card can be produced profitably, residents engage, and
advertisers renew or continue digitally.

Dashboards, custom billing, and advanced services should wait until the core
offer has paying demand.
